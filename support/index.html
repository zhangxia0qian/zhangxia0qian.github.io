What the App Does

WrapBrush: Car Studio helps you create, preview, and export PNG wrap images for supported vehicles that use Tesla's custom wraps workflow. The app does not connect to, control, diagnose, unlock, start, or modify any vehicle.

Export Help

  1. Create or open a design in the Design tab.
  2. Tap Export and enter the required file name suffix.
  3. Use the iOS share sheet to save the PNG file.
  4. Copy the PNG into a folder named Wraps on a compatible USB drive.
  5. Follow the in-app Setup Guide for vehicle-side loading steps.

Purchases and Restore

Premium access is handled through Apple's App Store payment system. If a purchase does not appear, open the subscription screen in the app and tap Restore Purchases. Monthly and yearly subscriptions can be managed through Apple ID subscription management.

Template Downloads

Free users can download one wrap template from the Template tab. Additional template downloads require an active monthly or yearly subscription, or a lifetime unlock purchase.
