Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 28, 2026

Tesla Wrap Editor ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information when you use it to create, preview, and export custom vehicle wrap images.

The App is an independent tool and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Tesla, Inc.

Information We Collect

The App is designed to work primarily on your device. We do not require an account, and we do not operate a server that automatically receives your designs, photos, stickers, exported files, or feedback.

The App may handle the following information locally on your device:

  • Photos or images you choose to import for wrap design.
  • Images captured using the camera, if you choose to use the camera feature.
  • Design settings such as selected vehicle model, colors, stickers, image position, scale, and rotation.
  • Feedback text, attachments, your email address, and diagnostic information only if you choose to send feedback by email.

Photos and Camera

If you grant photo library or camera access, the App uses that access only to let you select or capture images for your wrap design.

Imported photos are processed on your device. The App does not automatically upload your photos, generated designs, or exported PNG files to us.

Feedback

If you contact us by email from inside the App, your email address, message, attachments, and diagnostic information included in the email will be sent to us. The App does not automatically send feedback or diagnostic data without your action.

In-App Purchases

The current version of the App does not offer in-app purchases. If we add paid features in a future version, purchases will be processed by Apple through the App Store. We will update this Privacy Policy as needed.

Advertising and Tracking

The current version of the App does not show ads and does not track you across apps and websites owned by other companies.

If advertising or tracking features are added in a future version, we will update this Privacy Policy and the App Store privacy information before using those features.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

We do not share your photos, designs, exported PNG files, or feedback with third parties unless you choose to share them using iOS system sharing features or send them by email.

Data Storage

Most App data is stored locally on your device. You can delete locally stored App data by deleting the App from your device.

Exported files are saved or shared only when you choose to do so.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended to collect personal information from children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Third-Party Services

The App uses Apple system services, including iOS photo access, camera access, sharing features, Mail composer, and App Store distribution services. These services are governed by Apple's privacy practices.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the effective date at the top of this page.
